# Code of Conduct

> [English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [简体中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md#简体中文)

## Our Commitment

NoviqWiki contributors, maintainers, and community participants are committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, ability, appearance, or personal characteristics.

We will act in ways that support an open, respectful, and technically constructive community.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that supports the community include:

- Communicating respectfully and focusing criticism on ideas, code, and outcomes rather than people.
- Giving and accepting actionable feedback in good faith.
- Acknowledging mistakes, correcting them, and learning from the experience.
- Respecting privacy, boundaries, and differing levels of experience.
- Using inclusive language and helping other participants take part productively.
- Prioritizing the safety and long-term health of the project community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Discriminatory, sexualized, insulting, or demeaning language or imagery.
- Personal attacks, trolling, deliberate misrepresentation, or repeated bad-faith argument.
- Publishing another person's private information without explicit permission.
- Pressuring someone to reveal private, security-sensitive, or personal information.
- Retaliating against a person who raises a concern or participates in an investigation.
- Any other conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, commits, and other project-managed spaces. It also applies when someone officially represents NoviqWiki or its community in public spaces.

Security research performed under [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) must also follow this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting a Conduct Concern

Do not publish names, screenshots, private messages, or incident details in a public issue.

The repository does not currently publish a dedicated private conduct-reporting address. To request a private channel:

1. Use a private contact method shown on a repository maintainer's GitHub profile, if one is available; or
2. Open a minimal public issue that asks a maintainer to provide a private way to discuss a Code of Conduct concern. Do not identify the people involved or describe the incident in that issue.

A maintainer should move the conversation to a private channel before asking for details. A report should include, when safe and relevant, what happened, where and when it happened, available supporting material, and the outcome or protection requested.

If the concern involves a maintainer, say so in the private report and request that person not participate in handling it. If there is an immediate risk of physical harm, contact the appropriate local emergency service or platform safety team; this project cannot provide emergency response.

Use the vulnerability-reporting process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) only when the report also involves a security vulnerability.

## Enforcement

Maintainers who handle a report should:

- Limit details to the people needed to assess and resolve the concern.
- Consider the available context, impact, safety needs, and any pattern of behavior.
- Avoid retaliation and conflicts of interest.
- Communicate the outcome to the reporter when doing so is safe and appropriate.

Possible responses include a private correction, warning, removal of content, restriction from project spaces, or a permanent ban. Deliberately false reports or retaliation may also lead to enforcement. A report made in good faith is not a violation merely because the available evidence is incomplete.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) and adapted to the reporting channels currently documented by NoviqWiki.

## 简体中文

> [English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [简体中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md#简体中文)

### 我们的承诺

NoviqWiki 的贡献者、维护者和社区参与者致力于为所有人提供友好、安全且免受骚扰的环境，不因背景、身份、经验、能力、外貌或个人特征而区别对待。

我们会以有助于建设开放、尊重且重视技术协作的社区的方式行事。

### 期望行为

有助于社区建设的行为包括：

- 保持尊重，将批评集中在观点、代码和结果，而不是针对个人。
- 善意地提出和接受可执行的反馈。
- 承认并纠正错误，从中吸取经验。
- 尊重隐私、个人边界和经验水平差异。
- 使用包容性语言，帮助其他参与者有效参与。
- 优先考虑项目社区的安全和长期健康发展。

### 不可接受的行为

不可接受的行为包括：

- 骚扰、威胁、恐吓、跟踪或持续干扰。
- 歧视性、性化、侮辱性或贬损性的语言或图像。
- 人身攻击、恶意挑衅、故意歪曲或反复进行缺乏善意的争论。
- 未经明确许可公开他人的私人信息。
- 迫使他人披露私人、安全敏感或个人信息。
- 报复提出问题或参与调查的人。
- 任何理性人士会认为不适合专业社区的其他行为。

### 适用范围

本行为准则适用于仓库 issue、pull request、代码审查、讨论、提交以及其他由项目管理的空间。任何人在公共场合正式代表 NoviqWiki 或其社区时，本准则同样适用。

按照 [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) 开展的安全研究也必须遵守本行为准则。

### 报告行为问题

不要在公开 issue 中发布姓名、截图、私人消息或事件细节。

仓库目前没有公布专用的私密行为问题报告地址。如需请求私密沟通渠道：

1. 如果仓库维护者的 GitHub 个人资料提供了私密联系方式，请使用该方式；或
2. 创建一个内容最少的公开 issue，仅请求维护者提供私下讨论行为准则问题的方式。不要在该 issue 中指出相关人员或描述事件。

维护者应先将沟通转移到私密渠道，再询问详细信息。在安全且相关的情况下，报告应包括事件内容、发生时间和地点、可提供的支持材料，以及希望获得的处理结果或保护措施。

如果问题涉及某位维护者，请在私密报告中说明，并要求该人员不参与处理。如果存在人身伤害的紧迫风险，请联系当地相应的紧急服务或平台安全团队；本项目无法提供紧急响应。

仅当报告同时涉及安全漏洞时，才使用 [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) 中的漏洞报告流程。

### 执行

处理报告的维护者应：

- 仅向评估和解决问题所必需的人员提供相关细节。
- 综合考虑现有背景、影响、安全需求以及是否存在重复行为。
- 避免报复和利益冲突。
- 在安全且适当的情况下向报告人说明处理结果。

可能的处理方式包括私下纠正、警告、移除内容、限制进入项目空间或永久禁止参与。故意提交虚假报告或实施报复也可能受到处理。只要报告出于善意，即使现有证据不完整，也不会仅因此构成违规。
